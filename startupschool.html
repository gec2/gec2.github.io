IMMERSIVE BUSINESS TRAINING
FROM LEADING EXPERTS

At the core of our program lies the Customer Development Methodology - the world's fastest growing business development process. Developed by Steve Blank, the program focuses on three stages of business development: 1) Discovery/Startup, 2) Validation/Sales, and 3) Growth/Scale. We do not use case studies, you are the case study! We don't want you wasting time learning about someone else's business, we want you to grow yours! Whether you are looking to start a business, start a career in sales and business development, or marketing, we can get you the skills crafted by top pracademics in the field.

Customer Interviews

Develop the skills to talk with prospective customers to determine product-market-fit.

Pitching & Sales

Craft an effective pitch in order to educate potential customers on a product or service's value proposition.

Metrics

Analyze data, identify conversion rates, and trends by utilizing various tools.

Mentorship

Connect with subject-matter experts, investors, inventors, and other entrepreneurs to build your network.

Stodgy is out of style

Over 12 weeks you will learn what it takes to start, grow, and scale your business from leading entrepreneurs, executives, and investors. How we operate and launch new products has drastically changed over the past decade and you need to learn how to stay relevant to stay alive.
